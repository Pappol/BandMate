How It Works

Buffer Percentages: When calculating setlist time, new songs get extra time for learning, while mastered songs get minimal extra time for transitions.

Break Management: Automatic breaks are added for longer sessions to keep rehearsals productive and comfortable.

Time Clustering: Your requested session duration is automatically rounded to the nearest interval for better planning.

Example: A 3:45 song with 20% buffer becomes 4:30 in the setlist, giving you time to practice difficult parts.
